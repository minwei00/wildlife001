# Mandai Wildlife Reserve: Master Data

## 1. Singapore Zoo
- **Overview:** World’s best rainforest zoo, 4,200+ animals. 'Open concept' allows free-ranging orangutans.
- **Hours:** 8:30am – 6:00pm (Last Entry: 5:00pm)
- **Advisories:** Parking limited 30 May–1 Jun & 6–7 Jun 2026.
- **Highlights:** Into the Wild Presentation, Splash Safari, Animal Friends Presentation, Dance Your Heart Out (KidzWorld).
- **Itinerary Highlights:** African Herd (Feeding sessions for rhinos, zebras, giraffes), Fragile Forest biodome (butterflies, lemurs, sloths).

## 2. Night Safari
- **Overview:** World’s first nocturnal wildlife park. 900+ nocturnal creatures.
- **Hours:** 6:30pm – 12:00am (Last Entry: 11:15pm).
- **Advisories:** Time slot booking mandatory.
- **Highlights:** Safari Adventure Tram (7pm-11:20pm), Walking trails, Creatures of the Night presentation (7:30pm, 8:30pm, 9:30pm), Twilight Fire Spectacle.
- **Dining:** Ulu Ulu Safari Restaurant (5:30pm-11:00pm).

## 3. River Wonders
- **Overview:** Asia’s only river-themed wildlife park. 11,000+ animals.
- **Hours:** 9:00am – 6:00pm (Last Entry: 5:00pm)
- **Advisories:** Mekong/Yangtze exhibits partial closure for upgrades.
- **Highlights:** Amazon River Quest (boat ride, min height 1.06m), Giant Panda Forest (Jia Jia & Kai Kai), Amazon Flooded Forest (Manatees, Otters), Expert Aquarist talks.
- **Dining:** Mama Panda Kitchen (Panda-shaped buns).

## 4. Bird Paradise
- **Overview:** Asia’s largest bird park (17 hectares). 3,500+ birds, 8 walk-through aviaries.
- **Hours:** 9:00am – 6:00pm (Last Entry: 5:00pm).
- **Highlights:** Ocean Network Express Penguin Cove, Southern Cassowaries (Mysterious Papua), Predators on Wings presentation, American flamingos (Crimson Wetlands), Starlings feeding, Hornbill Keeper Talk.
- **Dining:** Crimson Restaurant (Lunch in the Wild 4-course meal, 11:30am-2pm), Penguin Cove Restaurant.

## 5. Rainforest Wild Adventure
- **Overview:** Asia’s first adventure-based wildlife park. 58 free-roaming species.
- **Hours:** 9:00am – 6:00pm (Last Entry: 5:00pm).
- **Highlights:** Canopy Glider, Ravine Swing, Wild Cavern Adventure, Wild Apex Adventure.
- **Itinerary Highlights:** The Karsts (Francois' langurs), Forest Treks (Malayan tiger), The Cavern (Cave racer snakes, Asian forest scorpions, The Oculus).

## 6. Curiosity Cove
- **Overview:** Indoor nature-inspired playscape for kids 12 and under.
- **Location:** Mandai Wildlife EAST.
- **Hours:** 10:00am – 6:30pm.

## 7. Exploria
- **Overview:** Multimedia-themed nature attraction featuring immersive digital expeditions.
- **Location:** Mandai Wildlife EAST.
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri: 11am–8pm | Sat–Sun & PH: 11am–9pm.
- **Five Worlds:** Interconnected World, Bitdeer AI, Infinite Wonderland, Micro Worlds, World of Darkness, Realm of the Giants.

## 8. Boardwalk & Gallery
- **Mandai Boardwalk:** 3.3km public walkway. Open 7am–7pm.
- **Mandai Gallery:** Exhibition on history. Located at Mandai Wildlife EAST. Open 10am–7pm.

## 9. Mandai Wildlife EAST & WEST
- **Mandai Wildlife EAST:** Wellness and dining hub. Open 7am–10:30pm.
- **Mandai Wildlife WEST:** Pet-friendly green space. Open 7am–10:30pm. Free parking 6pm–12am.

## 10. Visitor Essentials
- **Transport:** Accessible via public transport, Mandai Khatib Bus (M2), or driving.
- **Taxi Surcharge:** $5 surcharge for departures from Mandai Wildlife Reserve (1pm–11:59pm).
- **Rules:** No feeding, touching, or approaching wildlife. Smoke-free. No pets in ticketed areas.
- **App:** Mandai App for planning and navigation.
